History of Sri Gnana Saraswati Devasthanam Karamchedu

Gnana Saraswati Templeis a Hindu temple of Goddess Saraswati located on the banks of Godavari River at Basar, Telangana, India.[1] It is one of the two famous Saraswati temples in India, the other being in Jammu & Kashmir. Saraswati is the Hindu Goddess of knowledge and learning. Children are brought to the temple.

References have been made of this historical city in epics and many a literary works it was mentioned as ‘Dharmapura’ in Skandha and Brahmanda Puranas, Dharmapuri during Budhist period Dharmaram in Dekkala Banda inscription of the Rashtrakutas. Its name is also found in the writings of Ekamranatha of Kakatiya period.
